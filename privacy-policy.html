1. The Types of Personal Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information Collected Directly from You

KeyGoals may collect Personal Information directly from you when you use the Site or Online Services, including:

  • Identifiers, including name, telephone number, postal address, and email address
  • Information listed in the California Customer Records Statute, including name, postal address, and telephone number

  • Inferences generated from the goals you select and the information you provide to infer behavior, patterns, interests, and status about you

1.2 Personal Information Automatically Collected

KeyGoals and third-party service providers may automatically collect data about you when you use the Site. This Usage Data includes:

  • Identifiers, including IP address and cookies
  • Internet or Other Electronic Network Activity Information, including browsing history, browser and device information, hardware model, software, preferred language, unique device identifiers, advertising identifiers, network information, and clickstreams
  • Location information, including your location when you browse the Site or use the Online Services

1.3 Use of Cookies, Pixels, and Online Identifiers

We and our third-party providers use cookies, pixel tags, and other technologies to collect Usage Data. We use this Usage Data to analyze and understand how you access and interact with us, identify bugs and errors, optimize performance, and personalize content.

We use third-party analytics tools including:

  • Google Analytics — to help us analyze traffic and improve services. You can opt-out via Google's Ads Preferences Manager.
  • Meta Pixel — to measure, optimize, and retarget marketing campaigns. See Facebook's Data Policy for more information.
  • Google AdWords — for remarketing to previous visitors on third-party websites including Google.

1.4 Do-Not-Track

Many web browsers include a Do Not Track ("DNT") feature. However, there is currently no uniform standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals. As such, KeyGoals does not currently monitor or respond to DNT browser requests.

1.5 Information We Receive from Third Parties

We may collect Personal Information from third parties including affiliates, subsidiaries, data providers, and credit bureaus where permitted by law. We may also receive information about you from social media platforms when you interact with us on those platforms.

2. Purpose of Collection

KeyGoals processes Personal Information for the purposes of:

  • Providing the Online Services: Including tracking goal progress, providing predetermined actionable steps, and facilitating accountability reminders
  • Personalizing User Experience: Tailoring content based on your selected health, finance, and lifestyle goals
  • Facilitating Third-Party Advertising: Connecting users with local service providers based on selected goals and geographic location
  • Targeted Advertising and Lead Generation: Delivering personalized advertisements and promotional content through the Online Services
  • Monitoring and analyzing behavior: Reviewing user interaction with goal-setting tools and advertisements to improve service efficacy
  • Internal Business Operations: Conducting market research, data analytics, and development of new accountability features
  • CRM and Lead Management: Maintaining records of prospective customers and building mailing lists for product updates and partner promotions
  • Compliance and Safety: Meeting legal or regulatory requirements and enforcing KeyGoals's Terms of Service
