FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about advertising on Quest.

What does my ad actually look like in the app?

Your business name and logo appear as a sponsored banner inside the Quest app for all users browsing your chosen category. We'll work with you after sign-up to collect your logo and any tagline you'd like included.

How many users will see my ad?

Quest is in active growth with new users joining daily. All users who open a quest in your sponsored category will see your brand. We'll share usage stats as the platform scales.

Can I sponsor more than one category?

Yes — just submit the form once for each category you'd like to sponsor. Each slot is billed separately at $1/month during beta.

What happens when the beta ends?

Standard pricing is $20/slot/month. Early beta sponsors will be notified in advance and given the option to renew at beta pricing or cancel with no penalty. You'll never be charged a higher rate without explicit approval.

How do I cancel?

Email admin@questgoals.com and we'll cancel your subscription immediately. You can also manage your billing directly via the Stripe customer portal link we send with your invoice.

How does payment work?

After you submit this form, we'll send you a secure Stripe payment link. Your card is never stored by us — all billing is handled by Stripe. Monthly invoices are sent automatically.
